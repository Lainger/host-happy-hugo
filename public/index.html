About Host Happy

Hosting made simple.

You’re in full control of availability, rules and who is allowed in your space, we handle everything else.

We take care of the details, so you don’t have to.

We find, create, and manage one-of-a-kind vacation rentals in beautiful Victoria, British Columbia. Whether you already have a property or are searching for your perfect investment, we are local experts who can help you maximize your returns.

How It Works

Thinking of working with us but not sure how it works? No problem. We have a step-by-step process to answer all of your questions.

Still have questions? No problem - get in touch! We love to help.

Set-Up Buying
Step 1

Get an Estimate

When we first connect we will ask you a number of questions about your property to find out what your rough returns would look like if your property was converted to a short-term rental, or designed, styled and managed by us.

Step 2

Design Plan

Once we provide you with an estimate of how much you can expect to earn with us, we get perform an inspection of your property and develop a design customized to your space, your personal taste, and the expected target market for your space.
